About Me

I am Muhammad Danial Javed, a Mechanical Engineer with a strong interest in fluid mechanics, thermal systems, and renewable energy solutions. I graduated from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, where I gained expertise in computational simulations like CFD and FEA, alongside practical experience in mechanical design and energy optimization.


Currently, I am focused on leveraging fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and data-driven optimization to solve real-world problems, particularly in aerospace and energy-efficient systems. My skills include proficiency in Python, CAD/CAE tools, and applying Machine Learning for energy systems analysis. My goal is to drive innovation and contribute to sustainable solutions through research and development.
